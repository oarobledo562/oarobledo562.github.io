12 Months of...

Adobe Premiere

Tell a narrative of a year. Each of the 12 months needs to be represented by a corresponding video clip. Ensure that the video is two minutes in length and each individual clip may not exceed more than 10 seconds. Compose your video using Adobe Premiere video editing software.

I have decided to depict 12 months of nature in my video. Nature undergoes various transformations as the seasons progress and does so in a breath taking manner. In my video, I aimed to capture the diversity amd grandeur of nature while also demonstrating that change can be beautiful.
