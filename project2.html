Poster Design

Adobe Illustrator

Design a movie poster using Adobe Illustrator for a film adaptation of one of the following short stories: The Nine Billion Names of God by Arthur C. Clarke, The Secret Life of Walter Mitty by James Thurber, or The Garden Party by Katherine Mansfield. The final poster should include colorful and neat illustration that relates to scenes in the text, at least one symbol from the text, a catchy tagline, a release date, cast names, a title, and a director's name.
